Cornmeal Cake with Guava Paste

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 190 ml of milk (1 American glass)
  • 135 g of cornmeal (fub√°) (1 American glass)
  • 160 g of sugar (1 American glass)
  • 95 ml of oil (1/2 American glass)
  • 120 g of wheat flour (1 American glass)
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder (~15 g)
  • Guava paste in pieces
  • Fennel seeds to taste
Buy ingredients

Preparation Method

  1. Mix the egg, milk, oil, sugar, and cornmeal in a blender and beat well.
  2. Pour the mixture into a bowl, and mix with the flour and baking powder.
  3. Pour the batter into a pan greased with butter and flour, and add a layer of guava paste. Repeat this process until the pan is filled.
  4. Bake in a preheated medium oven ($180^{\circ}C$) for 40 minutes.
